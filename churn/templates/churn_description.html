Unsatisfied clients might express their will to leave a specific company (BRAND). This is what we call churn. We are listing here a list of basic example so you can understand it.

{#

Global roaming is now switched on

#}
No Source Text Label
1 BRAND-1 I am leaving <BRAND-1> at the end of the month! Churn
2 BRAND-1 I am fed up with <BRAND-1>! I’m switching to <BRAND-2>. Churn
3 BRAND-2 I am fed up with <BRAND-1>! I’m switching to <BRAND-2>. No churn
4 BRAND-1 I am fed up with <BRAND-1>! No churn


Note that for number 2 and 3, the text is the same. However th reference brand change. Indeed BRAND-2 is not considered by the churny content since the user wants to join its service . For example 4 it is not churn as well. Expressing disagreement with a service does not necessary mean you will quit it.

With the bot you can try yourself to enter text with either churny or non churny content. The aim of the bot will be to detect if the entry is churny or not. Please help us to improve bot performance by giving it feedback on its answers.
